1. Privacy Policy & Data Safety

At SmartGen, your privacy is our utmost priority. We are committed to protecting your data and ensuring a secure experience. Our approach to data handling is built on the principle of minimal data collection and maximum user control.

1.1. Local Data Processing & No Personal Data Collection

The core functionality of the SmartGen app and many of our web tools relies on local data processing. This means that for most utilities, such as the QR Code Generator, Image Compressor, Text Case Converter, and various calculators, all operations are performed directly on your device or within your web browser. Your input data (e.g., text for QR codes, images for compression) is processed client-side and typically never leaves your device or reaches our servers.

We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII) from our users. This includes, but is not limited to, your name, email address, location, IP address, device identifiers, or any other data that could directly identify you.

1.2. Data Safety Commitment

Our commitment to data safety includes:

  • Encryption: Where data might be temporarily stored (e.g., browser local storage for session management), it is handled securely.
  • Security Measures: We implement industry-standard security practices to protect our infrastructure and prevent unauthorized access to any non-personal, aggregated data we might collect for analytics.
  • Transparency: We aim to be fully transparent about our data practices, as detailed in this policy.

1.3. Data Retention Policy

Given our policy of not collecting personal data, there is generally no personal data for us to retain. For any non-personal, aggregated analytical data (e.g., app usage statistics, crash reports) collected via third-party services (see Section 1.4), this data is anonymized and retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, typically for performance analysis and service improvement. This retention period adheres to the policies of the respective third-party service providers.

1.4. Third-Party Services

We may integrate or use third-party services to enhance app functionality, analyze performance, or display advertisements. These services may have their own privacy policies regarding the data they collect. Examples include:

  • Google Analytics for Firebase: Used for anonymous app usage analytics and crash reporting to help us improve the app. This data is aggregated and does not identify individual users.
  • Google AdSense/AdMob: For displaying advertisements within the app and on our web platforms. Please refer to Section 2 for detailed information on advertising.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers for more information on their data handling practices.

2. Advertising & AdSense Policy

To keep SmartGen 100% free and accessible to everyone, we may utilize advertising networks, including Google AdSense and Google AdMob, to display advertisements within our mobile application and on our associated web tools (smartgentools.com).

2.1. Personalized Ads & Cookies

These advertising services may use technologies such as cookies, device identifiers, and similar tracking technologies to collect data about your interests and interactions with our app and other websites/apps. This information is used to serve you with personalized advertisements that are more relevant to your interests.

Cookies: A cookie is a small piece of data stored on your device by your web browser or app. It allows websites and apps to remember information about your browsing habits or device. You have control over cookies through your browser settings.

2.2. User Control & Opt-Out

You have options to control or opt-out of personalized advertising:

  • Ad Settings: You can visit Google's Ad Settings to manage the ads you see and opt-out of personalized ads from Google.
  • Device Settings: On your mobile device, you can often reset your advertising ID or limit ad tracking through your device's system settings.
  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings.

Please note that opting out of personalized ads does not mean you will no longer see advertisements; it simply means the ads you see may be less relevant to your interests.

2.3. Future Compliance

We are committed to complying with all relevant advertising policies, including Google's Publisher Policies and the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) where applicable. We continuously monitor changes in regulations and update our practices accordingly.

The SmartGen app may request certain permissions to function correctly. We believe in full transparency regarding why these permissions are needed:

SmartGen is designed to offer flexibility and utility both online and offline:

We strive to maximize offline usability while providing enhanced features when connected.
