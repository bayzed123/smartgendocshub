Why Your Website Needs a Privacy Policy

A Privacy Policy is a legal document that discloses some or all of the ways a party gathers, uses, discloses, and manages a customer or client's data. It fulfills a legal requirement to protect a customer or client's privacy. In many jurisdictions, including the EU (GDPR) and California (CCPA), having a clear and accessible privacy policy is not just a best practice—it's the law. Even if you aren't legally required to have one, providing a privacy policy builds trust with your users by being transparent about how you handle their information.
