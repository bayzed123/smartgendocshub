Protect Your Business with Professional Terms & Conditions

Terms and Conditions (also known as Terms of Service or Terms of Use) are a set of rules and regulations that users must agree to follow in order to use your website or mobile app. While not legally required like a Privacy Policy, having a T&C agreement is highly recommended for any business. It allows you to set the rules for using your service, protect your intellectual property, and limit your legal liability in case of disputes.

Why Use Our T&C Generator?

  • Legal Protection: Define user behavior and protect your business from misuse.
  • IP Protection: Clearly state your ownership of the website's content and trademarks.
  • Liability Limitation: Include disclaimers to reduce your legal risk.
  • User Trust: Professional terms show that you take your business and your users seriously.
