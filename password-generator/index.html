Why Use a Password Generator?

Using weak or reused passwords is one of the biggest security risks online. A random password generator creates complex strings of characters that are nearly impossible for hackers to guess or "brute force."

What Makes a Password Strong?

A strong password should be at least 12-16 characters long and include a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and special symbols. The more random the password, the more secure your account will be.
