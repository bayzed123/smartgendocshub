Comprehensive Privacy Policy for SmartGen

Last Updated: May 29, 2026

At SmartGen (smartgentools.com), we are deeply committed to protecting the privacy of our users. This Comprehensive Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect, how we use it, the stringent measures we take to safeguard your data, and your rights concerning your personal information. Our commitment extends to full compliance with global data protection regulations, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), as well as the strict requirements set forth by Google AdSense for publishers.

We believe in transparency and empowering our users with clear, understandable information about their privacy. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your data.

1. Key Principles of SmartGen's Privacy Philosophy

Our approach to user privacy is built upon several foundational principles that guide every aspect of SmartGen's operation:

  • Client-Side Processing: The vast majority of our tools operate entirely within your web browser. This means that the data you input into tools like our JSON Formatter, Base64 Encoder, or various calculators is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers. We do not see, store, or have access to this sensitive input data.
  • Minimal Data Collection: We are committed to collecting only the absolute minimum amount of data necessary to operate our website, provide our services, and ensure a secure and functional user experience.
  • Transparency: We strive to be completely open about our data practices. This policy details what data is collected, why it's collected, and how it's used.
  • User Control: We empower our users with control over their data, providing clear mechanisms for managing cookie preferences and understanding their rights under applicable privacy laws.
  • Security by Design: Our platform is engineered with security in mind, implementing measures to protect against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction of any data we do handle.

2. Information We DO NOT Collect or Store

A cornerstone of SmartGen's privacy-first design is our explicit commitment to not collect, store, or transmit the following types of information:

  • Personal Data Input into Tools: When you use any of our client-side utility tools (e.g., text converters, calculators, QR code generators, hash generators, etc.), the data you enter or upload for processing remains on your device. It is never sent to our servers, nor is it stored in any database controlled by SmartGen. This ensures that sensitive information, proprietary code, or personal calculations you perform using our tools remain entirely private to you.
  • User Accounts or Registrations: SmartGen does not require users to create accounts, log in, or provide any personal identification to access our tools. This eliminates the need to collect and store usernames, passwords, email addresses, or other account-related personal data.
  • Direct Personal Identifiers: We do not actively seek to collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, physical addresses, or other direct personal identifiers from our users, except when you voluntarily provide them (e.g., via our contact form, as detailed below).

Our primary goal is to offer valuable utilities without compromising your privacy or requiring you to trust us with your sensitive information. The client-side nature of our tools is a testament to this commitment.

3. Information We DO Collect (and Why)

While we prioritize client-side processing, certain limited types of information are collected to ensure the functionality, security, and improvement of SmartGen. This collection is always performed with user privacy in mind and in compliance with relevant regulations.

3.1. Log Files

Like many other websites, SmartGen makes use of log files. These files record visitors to the site, typically as part of hosting services' analytics. The information inside the log files includes:

  • Internet Protocol (IP) addresses
  • Browser type
  • Internet Service Provider (ISP)
  • Date and time stamp
  • Referring/exit pages
  • Possibly the number of clicks

This information is used for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information. IP addresses and other such information are not linked to any information that is personally identifiable. This data helps us understand how our website is being used, identify potential security threats, and improve overall site performance and user experience. It is processed in an aggregated and anonymized manner wherever possible.

3.2. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

SmartGen uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience, provide necessary website functionality, serve relevant advertisements, and analyze our traffic. By continuing to use our website, you consent to the use of cookies in accordance with this Privacy Policy. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings at any time, and we provide a clear cookie consent mechanism on our site.

3.2.1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site. Cookies can be used by websites to make a user's experience more efficient. The law states that we can store cookies on your device if they are strictly necessary for the operation of this site. For all other types of cookies, we need your permission.

3.2.2. How SmartGen Uses Cookies

We use cookies for several purposes:

  • Essential Website Functionality: These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off in our systems. They are usually only set in response to actions made by you which amount to a request for services, such as setting your privacy preferences, logging in, or filling in forms. You can set your browser to block or alert you about these cookies, but some parts of the site will not then work.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies allow us to count visits and traffic sources so we can measure and improve the performance of our site. They help us to know which pages are the most and least popular and see how visitors move around the site. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. If you do not allow these cookies, we will not know when you have visited our site and will not be able to monitor its performance.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies may be set through our site by our advertising partners. They may be used by those companies to build a profile of your interests and show you relevant adverts on other sites. They do not store directly personal information but are based on uniquely identifying your browser and internet device. If you do not allow these cookies, you will experience less targeted advertising.

3.3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdSense)

To keep SmartGen 100% free and accessible to all users, we display advertisements provided by third-party vendors, primarily Google AdSense. Google AdSense is one of the largest advertising networks globally, and its operation involves the use of cookies and other tracking technologies to serve relevant ads to our users.

  • Google as a Third-Party Vendor: Google uses cookies to serve ads on SmartGen. Google's use of the DART cookie enables it to serve ads to our users based on their visit to SmartGen and other sites on the Internet.
  • DoubleClick DART Cookie: The DoubleClick DART cookie is used by Google in the ads served on publisher websites displaying AdSense for content ads. When users visit an AdSense publisher's website and either view or click on an ad, a cookie may be dropped on that end user's browser. The data gathered from these cookies is used to help AdSense publishers serve and manage ads on their site(s) and across the web.
  • Opting Out of DART Cookies: Users may opt out of the use of the DART cookie by visiting the Google Ad and Content Network Privacy Policy at the following URL: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads. Alternatively, you can opt out of some third-party vendors' use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting www.aboutads.info/choices.
  • Personalized Advertising: Google AdSense may use personalized advertising. This means that ads you see may be based on your interests and past interactions. We are committed to ensuring that all advertising on SmartGen adheres to the Better Ads Standards and Google's publisher policies, avoiding intrusive or deceptive ad formats.
  • AdSense Policy Compliance: As required by Google AdSense Program Policies, we explicitly disclose our use of Google AdSense, the types of data collected by Google for advertising purposes (e.g., through DART cookies), and how users can manage their ad preferences or opt-out. We ensure that our privacy policy is easily accessible and clearly explains these practices to our users.

3.4. Other Third-Party Services

SmartGen may use other third-party services for analytics, security, or to enhance website functionality. These third parties may also use cookies and similar tracking technologies. We only engage with reputable third-party service providers who adhere to high standards of data protection and privacy. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third parties for more information on their data practices.

5. Data Retention

SmartGen retains collected information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements. Generally, log file data and anonymized analytics data are retained for a period of up to 26 months, in line with standard industry practices and Google Analytics data retention policies. Information related to advertising cookies is retained according to the policies of our advertising partners (e.g., Google AdSense).

Since we do not collect or store personal data input into our client-side tools, there is no retention period for such data on our servers.

6. Data Security

The security of your data is paramount to us. While SmartGen's client-side processing model inherently minimizes server-side data exposure, for the limited data we do handle (e.g., log files, anonymized analytics), we implement robust security measures. These include:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your browser and our servers (e.g., for loading website resources) is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology.
  • Access Controls: Access to any collected data is restricted to authorized personnel only, who are bound by strict confidentiality obligations.
  • Regular Security Audits: We regularly review our security practices and infrastructure to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Minimization of Data: By design, we collect and retain the least amount of data necessary, thereby reducing the potential impact of any security breach.

Despite our efforts, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

9. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location, you may have specific rights regarding your personal data. SmartGen is committed to facilitating the exercise of these rights in compliance with applicable laws, including GDPR and CCPA.

9.1. General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Rights

If you are a resident of the European Economic Area (EEA) or the UK, you have certain data protection rights. SmartGen aims to take reasonable steps to allow you to correct, amend, delete, or limit the use of your Personal Data. Your principal rights under GDPR are:

  • The right to access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data. We may charge you a small fee for this service.
  • The right to rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete the information you believe is incomplete.
  • The right to erasure: You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to restrict processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to object to processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • The right to data portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
  • The right to withdraw consent: You also have the right to withdraw your consent at any time where SmartGen relied on your consent to process your personal information.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at cwb.agency@outlook.com.

9.2. California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident, the CCPA provides you with specific rights regarding your personal information. Your principal rights under CCPA are:

  • The right to know: You have the right to request that a business disclose what personal information it collects, uses, discloses, and sells.
  • The right to delete: You have the right to request the deletion of your personal information collected or maintained by the business.
  • The right to opt-out: You have the right to opt-out of the sale of your personal information. SmartGen does not sell personal information.
  • The right to non-discrimination: You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising any of your CCPA rights.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at cwb.agency@outlook.com. We will respond to your request within 45 days.
