What is an Age Calculator?

An age calculator is a tool that determines the interval between two dates. Most commonly, it's used to find out exactly how many years, months, and days old a person is based on their birth date.

How the Calculation Works

Our tool takes your birth date and compares it with the current date, accounting for leap years and the varying number of days in each month to provide a precise breakdown of your age.

Features

  • Precise years, months, and days
  • Real-time calculation
  • Clean, easy-to-read interface

FAQs

Is my birth date private?

Yes, the calculation is done entirely in your browser. We do not store or transmit your personal dates.

Can I calculate future dates?

This tool is optimized for calculating age from the past to the present.

Does it handle leap years?

Yes, our JavaScript-based logic correctly handles the extra day in February during leap years.
