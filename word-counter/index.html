Why Use a Word Counter?

Whether you're writing a blog post, an academic essay, or a social media update, keeping track of your word and character count is essential. Many platforms have strict limits, and search engines often prefer content of a certain length.

Estimating Reading Time

Our tool uses an average reading speed of 200 words per minute to estimate how long it will take for a typical reader to finish your content. This is a great metric to include at the top of your blog posts to improve user experience.

Features

  • Real-time counting
  • Character count (with and without spaces)
  • Accurate reading time estimation
